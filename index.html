Flair and Keys is a French company that helps non-resident buyers customize and purchase their dream property in France.

Foreigners can be confused when it comes to finding their perfect home in France. Retirement plan, pied-à-terre in France, investment, people all have their reasons to live their French dream. Flair and Keys is here to help unlock creative possibilities.

Flair and Keys is specialized in complete refurbishment and upgrades of housing in uncommon places (barn, loft, house, chateau, factory), with a French savoir-faire in line with customer’s needs.

Flair and Keys is a web platform that connect buyers with interior designers for an invitation to submit turnkey solutions. This competitive stage and special commercial conditions with local artisans allow buyers to optimize their budget.
